Dutco Balfour Beatty (DBB) was formally incorporated in 1984. Over the years, the Dutco Balfour Beatty has evolved into one of the leading civil, heavy civil and marine contractors in the UAE with a well-established record for timely delivery and customer satisfaction.

The company maintains a signiﬁcant force of skilled personnel led by highly qualiﬁed engineers.
